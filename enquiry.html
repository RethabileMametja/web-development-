Frequently Asked Questions

Common Questions About the Garden

Do I need gardening experience?

Not at all! We welcome gardeners of all skill levels. Our experienced members and regular workshops will help you learn everything you need to know.

What if I travel frequently?

Community members can help care for your plot when you're away. We have a buddy system and plot-sharing arrangements for frequent travelers.

Are there any costs involved?

Membership is free! Plot holders pay a small annual fee to cover water and maintenance, but all other membership levels are completely free.

Can I bring my children?

Absolutely! We encourage families to join. We have a dedicated children's area and family-friendly events throughout the year.
